About Us

What do you want to know?

Welcome to Happy Hoopla!—where the magic of the circus comes alive and happiness knows no bounds! At Happy Hoopla, we're all about bringing joy, laughter, and wonder to audiences of all ages. Our story began with a dream to create a magical space where the thrill of the circus meets the warmth of community. With a colorful history and a troupe of talented performers, we've become a beloved destination for families, friends, and fun-seekers alike.

Incredible Animals: From stunning horses to clever dogs, our animals dazzle the crowd with tricks and stunts you won't want to miss!
Laugh-Out-Loud Clowns: Our clowns are experts in mischief, bringing endless fun with their pranks and hilarious acts.
Enchanting Dancers: Feel the magic as our dancers take the stage, adding elegance and excitement with every step.

Our journey began with a spark of creativity and a dash of daring. From the inception of Happy Hoopla, we've been weaving a tapestry of wonder that combines mystery with joy. Each performance is crafted with an element of surprise, inviting you to discover something new each time.

What sets us apart at Happly Hoopla is our unique blend of traditional circus magic with innovative twists, making each performance a new adventure. We offer interactive experiences that invite the audience to engage directly with our acts. Our shows cater to all ages, ensuring fun and excitement for everyone. Above all, we foster a sense of community, creating joyful moments and lasting memories.

Our Services

Can-Can

Our dazzling dancers, adorned in lavish, colorful costumes, kick, twirl, and leap across the stage in perfect unison, delivering a heart-racing performance that will leave you spellbound. With lively music and electrifying routines, this show radiates joy, excitement, and circus magic at every turn. It's an unforgettable celebration of rhythm and grace, where the stage comes alive with the thrill of the can-can!

Elephant On A Ball

As the lights dim and the circus tent fills with anticipation, the spotlight falls on the elephant, who delicately places one foot on the brightly colored ball. With perfect precision, the elephant balances effortlessly, captivating the audience with every subtle movement. This act is not only a display of physical strength but also of deep trust between the elephant and its trainers.

Fire Hoop Jump

Unleash the excitement with our Tiger Fire Hoop Services, where danger meets dazzling artistry! Watch in awe as our magnificent tigers leap through fiery hoops, showcasing their strength and grace in a breathtaking display that will leave your audience gasping. This electrifying performance combines the thrill of the wild with the artistry of circus tradition, creating an unforgettable spectacle!.

Puppy Ramp Adventure

Bring the joy of the circus to your event with our Puppy Ramp Adventure Services! Our delightful puppies will dazzle guests as they navigate colorful ramps and obstacles, showcasing their playful spirit and agility. Perfect for family-friendly events, parties, and festivals, this engaging attraction creates unforgettable moments filled with laughter and charm.

Human Towers

Transform your event with our captivating Human Tower Services! Experience the thrill as our acrobatic team constructs awe-inspiring human towers, showcasing incredible skill and strength. Each formation tells a story of balance and bravery, mesmerizing audiences and creating unforgettable moments. Perfect for any occasion,this stunning performance brings an electrifying energy!

Camel Show

Immerse yourself in the excitement of our Camel Dance and Stunt Spectacle! Experience the magic as our trained camels take center stage, dazzling audiences with their rhythmic dances, playful antics, and jaw-dropping stunts. Each performance is a vibrant celebration of culture and talent, showcasing the unique bond between the camels and their trainers.
